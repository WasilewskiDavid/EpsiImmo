Mentions légales

Earthbound was founded in 2000 by a seasoned team of successful entrepreneurs with deep experience in the world of retail, brand licensing, and product design and development. Their significant business success in diverse but related fields, paired with their groundbreaking approach to licensing, set the stage for great success in the world of direct to retail licensing. Within its first 2 years Earthbound placed and launched the breakthrough partnership between Isaac Mizrahi and Target. Since then, Earthbound has placed and managed direct to retail and brand licensing programs with the world's largest retailers that have generated more than $16 billion in retail sales.
